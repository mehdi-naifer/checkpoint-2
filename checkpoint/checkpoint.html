Forest

Seasons are periods of the year with distinct weather conditions and day.
The four seasons — winter, spring, summer, autumn — can vary significantly in characteristics and can prompt changes in the world around them.
Here, we explore the seasons of the year in more detail. Attributes of the seasons may vary by location, but there are still broad definitions that cross most of the boundaries.

winter forest

How beautiful the winter forest looks and how good it is for a walk! Everything around is white, covered with soft fluffy snow.

spring forest

Spring. The most beautiful time of the year. The first warm rays of the sun. The sound of running water from under the melted snow.

summer forest

Summer Forest – one must be able to see its beauty. summer vacation! Summer freedom! How pleasant are the walks through the summer forest.

autumn forest

Autumn is the season when animals, people, and nature prepare for winter. Leaves turn orange, yellow, or brown. The weather becomes chillier,mist and fog will appear more often.
